Empowering Your Child’s Journey from the First Step

Igniting Bright Beginnings with Our Play School Programs!


At our pre-primary school, we have designed personalised learning journeys for preschool kids that ensure a holistic and engaging educational experience meant exclusively for nurturing their creativity and curiosity. Our baby play school has four programs, namely—Playgroup (Level 1), Nursery (Level 2), LKG (Level 3), and UKG (Level 4).

Playgroup
Our Playgroup program helps children explore and learn through fun activities.
Nursery
Exploring creativity through interactive learning.
LKG
Our LKG program prepares your child for formal schooling with a focus on language and cognitive skills.
UKG
Preparing for primary education with confidence.

Why You Choose Viva Kids World?
